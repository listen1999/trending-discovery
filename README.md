# Trending Discovery

Daily intelligence reports generated from curated Twitter lists, covering AI trends, one-person company insights, and cross-domain thought signals.

## Reports

Three reports are generated daily from the past 24 hours of tweets:

| Report | Focus | Audience |
|--------|-------|----------|
| `opc.md` | AI 一人公司日报 | Solopreneurs, indie hackers, one-person company founders |
| `ai_trend.md` | AI 行业情报简报 | AI practitioners, engineers, product managers |
| `thought.md` | 思想发现简报 | Investors, strategists, cross-domain thinkers |

## Structure

```
2026/
└── 05/
    ├── 09/
    │   ├── opc.md
    │   ├── ai_trend.md
    │   └── thought.md
    ├── 10/
    └── 11/
```

Reports are organized by `year/month/day/`.

## Data Sources

- **opc**: Twitter list tracking indie makers, solopreneurs, and bootstrapped founders
- **ai_trend**: Twitter list tracking AI researchers, labs, and industry leaders
- **thought**: Twitter list tracking investors, intellectuals, and cross-domain thinkers

## Language

Reports are written in Chinese (中文) with English technical terms where appropriate.
